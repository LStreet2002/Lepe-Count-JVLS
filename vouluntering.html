Volunteering

Information

With over a mile of beach, pine fringed cliffs, wildflower meadows, woods and historic D-Day remains, Lepe Country Park is the ideal place to relax and watch the world go by.

A number of different volunteer opportunities are available here: ranger – helping the rangers to carry out the conservation management front of house – welcoming visitors to the site and helping to provide customer service, care and security for the benefit of our visitors and the local community engagement volunteer – helping out at events, guiding people around the park, being on hand to answer questions and talking to the public
