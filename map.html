The Beach

The shore stretches for miles in both directions, and the soft sand is perfect for long walks in the evening.

The Field

Overlooking the beach there is a large patch of green, perfect for picnics and sports. There are benches and a park for relaxing on.

The Rivers

Opposing some stretches of the beach lies some grassland, rivers and other bodies of water. They are much calmer than the sea, and have some fish close to the shore

The Sea

Sweeping the length of the park are the cool blue waters of the Solent. There is very little wildlife close to the beach, however there are a few smaller fish further out
