Lepe Loop

This is a 5 mile coast and countryside circular walk around Lepe and Exbury. Dogs can also be taken on the route which will take around 2 hours at a leisurely pace. During Winter the paths can be boggy so wellingtons and sensible outdoor clothing are recommended. At high tide, there is an alternative route following the lane.
