Our ingredients

We’re passionate about Hampshire and want to give our customers a taste of what the county has to offer. That's why we look locally for inspiration when creating our menus. We use local fruit and vegetables when in season Our free range eggs are from Fordingbridge, Hampshire We source our fish products from sustainable, well-managed fisheries Our Red Tractor chicken breast and RSPCA assured pork loin are UK reared We’re proud to support local business too We serve Mozzo coffee, roasted in Southampton to our own special recipe.

Dogs welcome

Dogs are welcome in the Lookout We just ask some simple ‘petiquette’ for our lovely 4 legged friends:

Your dog is clean and flea free.
Muddy paws like muddy shoes should be wiped
Your dog is house trained and trained to sit
You always clean up after your dog
Your dog doesn't disturb others by barking
Your dog does not jump up at or pester other people
You never leave your dog unattended or unsupervised
